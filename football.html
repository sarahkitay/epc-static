    Academics

    1:5 Tutoring • Study Skills

    • Format: Small-group tutoring (qualified educators)
    • Cadence: Daily blocks with progress tracking
    • Focus: Core classes, test prep, eligibility

    How it works: Families choose a homeschool program from our list of vetted vendors, each offering unique perks such as yearly stipends, online curriculum, or live teacher options. This ensures the best fit for every student.

    Once school is selected, our team builds individual learning plans with weekly checkpoints and parent updates. We protect academic momentum while developing habits that last.

    Performance Training

    Two Sessions / Day

    • Frequency: 4 days per week
    • Session 1: Speed or skill (position-specific)
    • Session 2: Weight room (strength/power)
    • Oversight: DPT/DC prevention integrated

    Speed/skill work paired with a strength block creates balanced development. Clinical oversight reduces injury risk and accelerates progress with smart progression.

    Recovery

    Physical Therapy • Contrast • Normatec • Bodywork

    • Therapies: Physical therapy & injury prevention
    • Modalities: Contrast therapy (sauna + cold plunge) & Normatec boots
    • Bodywork: Assisted stretching, Theragun, and massage

    Recovery is scheduled into the week so athletes adapt, not just grind. Clinical guidance ensures the right dose of work, restoration, and soft-tissue care.

    What's Included

    • Academics with 1:5 tutoring and weekly progress updates (Full-Time School Academy)
    • Two daily training sessions (performance + skills)
    • Meal prep: daily lunch + snack for training days (Full-Time School Academy)
    • Clinical prevention & recovery (PT, contrast, Normatec, stretching, Theragun, massage)
