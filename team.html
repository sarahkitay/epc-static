Our Team

Dedicated professionals committed to your success

SASHA KHOSHABEH

CEO & CO-FOUNDER

A former professional soccer player, Sasha's journey in sports was cut short by a career-ending injury. Drawing from his personal experiences with injuries and recovery, he envisioned a space that offered comprehensive wellness services under one roof.

This passion project led to the creation of Elite Performance Clinic, where he has assembled a team of top-tier professionals to deliver exceptional care. Sasha's dedication to helping others achieve their best selves drives everything we do.

ALEX REYES, MPT

SUPERVISOR & MENTOR

Alex Reyes, MPT, OCS is a highly regarded physical therapist with a strong background in orthopedic care, advanced manual therapy, and spinal mobilization. A graduate of UCLA and USC, he became a Certified Orthopedic Clinical Specialist in 2003 and has held leadership roles including Clinical Specialist at Kaiser West L.A. and Director of Physical Therapy at Century City Doctors Hospital.

At EPC, Alex consults with athletes from the NBA, NFL, NCAA, and high school levels, specializing in injury assessment, rehabilitation planning, and mentorship of trainers. With over 30 years of martial arts practice, he blends science, movement, and holistic principles to help patients and athletes reach their highest potential.

ARTIN

PHYSICAL THERAPY STUDENT, AIDE & TRAINER

Artin is a dedicated physical therapy student and trainer who brings passion and commitment to every session. Working as both a physical therapy aide and performance trainer, Artin combines clinical knowledge with practical application to support clients in their recovery and performance goals.

With hands-on experience in rehabilitation protocols and training methodologies, Artin helps bridge the gap between therapy and performance, ensuring clients receive comprehensive care throughout their journey.

SANDY YEGHIKIAN

PT AIDE

Sandy is a NASM-certified personal trainer with nearly a decade of experience in fitness and rehabilitation. As a passionate mother of two, she is dedicated to helping others achieve their fitness goals.

Sandy's dual role as a physical therapy aide allows her to support clients in both their fitness journeys and rehabilitation, combining her expertise with her enthusiastic, caring approach to help clients reach their full potential.

OUR TEAM

Misha Cavaye

PT Aide & Performance Coach

Originally from London, England, Misha brings 14 years of professional basketball experience to EPC, including the last 8 seasons as a strength and conditioning coach for the South Bay Lakers. He specializes in postural correction, biomechanics, and nervous system management, utilizing integrative, holistic methods to optimize overall body function. Certified in advanced techniques including PRI, Myodetox, FRC, DNS, NKT, and AIM.

Sarah Kitay

Performance Coach

Sarah is a NASM-certified personal trainer with a focus on weight loss and hypertrophy. She combines evidence-based strength training principles with functional movement techniques to help clients sculpt lean muscle, improve performance, and build sustainable habits.

Chris Aguado

Performance Coach

Coach Aguado has a strong focus on sport specific strength training and has been a guide for some of the most impressive up and coming sports stars to date.

Simon Angell

Physical Therapy Assistant

Simon is a licensed physical therapy assistant who also works with UCLA Athletics, bringing high-level clinical experience to every session. His knowledge of rehab protocols and athlete care helps bridge the gap between therapy and performance.

Ray Williams II

Performance Coach

Ray is a certified personal trainer known for his patient, supportive coaching style and natural ability to connect with teens and young athletes. With a strong foundation in strength training and athletic development, he helps clients build confidence, discipline, and long-term fitness habits.

Eliel Swinton

Speed & Performance Coach

Coach Eliel Swinton is a former Stanford football standout and elite speed coach with more than 15 years of experience developing athletes across multiple sports. Known for his precision-based sprint mechanics and movement efficiency training, he helps clients enhance acceleration, agility, and game-speed performance.

Joshua James

Founder, F.U.S.E. Training

Josh is a movement specialist and founder of F.U.S.E. Training with over 15 years of experience in functional strength and injury prevention. After injuries cut short his athletic career, he shifted focus to longevity, learning how to heal, strengthen, and prevent injury through proper mechanics. His methodology centers on primal movement patterns and building balanced, individualized programs that address the body as a global system.

Brian Stevenson

Founder, Built Fit Co.

Brian is the founder of Built Fit Co., a strength and conditioning specialist focused on helping clients build sustainable fitness habits through intelligent programming. With a background in athletic performance and functional training, Brian designs programs that prioritize movement quality, strength development, and long-term health. His coaching philosophy emphasizes building confidence, consistency, and capability.
