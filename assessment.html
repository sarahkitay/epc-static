The Assessment Process

1

Pedics Machine Analysis

Your assessment begins with our Pedics machine, which analyzes your gait and squat patterns. This advanced technology provides detailed insights into your movement mechanics and can create custom insoles (the same kind used by many professional athletes) to optimize your foundation and support proper alignment.

2

Proteus Machine Testing

Next, you'll move to our Proteus machine: the only machine capable of numerically quantifying the amount of power you output. This revolutionary technology provides objective data on your strength, power, and asymmetries, establishing a precise baseline for your performance capabilities.

3

Functional Movement Screening & Range of Motion Testing

Our clinical team conducts comprehensive functional movement screening and range of motion testing to identify movement patterns, restrictions, and potential injury risk factors. This detailed analysis reveals how your body moves and where imbalances or limitations may exist.

4

Outdoor Speed & Agility Assessment

Finally, you'll complete your outdoor speed and agility assessment with Coach Eliel. This real-world performance evaluation tests your athletic capabilities in a dynamic environment, providing crucial data on your speed, agility, change of direction, and movement quality under functional conditions.
